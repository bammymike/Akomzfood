Health & Nutritional Benefits Of Eating Mushrooms

Lower Cholesterol

Mushrooms have zero Cholesterol or fat and are low in carbohydrates. However, they are rich in lean protein, which helps to burn cholesterol. They also contain fibre and enzymes that reduce cholesterol levels.

Anemia Treatment

Mushrooms contain lots of iron, which is necessary for the production of red blood cells, making them a good choice in the treatment of anemia.

Mushrooms contain high amounts of selenium, vitamin D, and vitamin B6. Selenium can help prevent cell damage in our bodies, vitamin D helps with cell growth, and vitamin B6 helps our bodies form red blood cells. All of these nutrients in mushrooms helps to maintain a healthy immune system.

Cancer Prevention

Mushrooms contain properties, beta-glucans and conjugated linoleic acid, which are essentially anti-carcinogenic, helping in the prevention of breast and prostrate cancer.

Diabetes Prevention

Because they ahve no cholesterol or fat, are low on carbohydrates, and contain lots of protein, minerals and vitamins, mushrooms are the perfect meal for people living with diabetes. Additionally, they contain insulin and certain compounds which assist in the production of insulin in the body.
