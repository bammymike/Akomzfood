About Us

We grow and sell specifically mushrooms scientifically known as agaricus bisporus

At Akomz Foods we grow and sell mushrooms--specifically mushrooms scientifically known as agaricus bisporus.
These mushrooms come in three varieties: white button mushrooms, ceremi, and portabello. The difference in these mushrooms as to do with their age when they are harvested.
White button mushrooms are the youngest at the time of harvest. This is the most consumed variety of mushroom in the world. As their name imply, they are white; however, the ones that grow in the wild tend to have a light brown tint to them. Button mushrooms are also called table mushrooms.
The older variety to the white button mushroom, harvested much later, is the cremini mushroom. it's color is brown, it's texture firmer, and it's taste more flavorful, because it is allowed to grow longer before harvest. The creminimushroom is also known as a baby bella.
Another strain of agaricus bisporus similar in look to the cremini mushroom is the chestnut mushroom. Like the cremini mushroom, it's color is brown.
When allowed to grow for even longer, the cremini becomes the portobello mushroom. By now, it's fully mature. At this point, it gains more flavour and the texture is considerably firmer. Due to it's shape, the portobello mushroom is sometimes called the open cap mushroom.
Our button mushrooms are grown at our farm, and proccessed and well-packaged at our factory, in Ogun State Nigeria.
